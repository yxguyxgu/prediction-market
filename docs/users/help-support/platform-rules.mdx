## Community and platform expectations

To keep markets fair and usable, <SiteName /> expects users to:

- avoid spam, fraud, or deceptive behavior
- avoid manipulative misuse of community/support channels
- provide honest context when escalating disputes/support requests
- follow applicable terms and regional restrictions

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warning, feature restrictions, or account suspension depending on severity.

## Report abuse

If you observe suspicious behavior, report with evidence (links, tx hash, screenshots).
