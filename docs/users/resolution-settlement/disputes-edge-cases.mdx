## How disputes appear in the product

When applicable, the market timeline shows statuses such as:

- <strong>Dispute window</strong>
- <strong>Disputed</strong>
- <strong>Final review</strong>

During active dispute windows, <SiteName /> can show a <strong>Dispute</strong> action
that links to the oracle dispute flow.

## Practical steps for users

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Read market rules first

    Confirm the exact resolution criteria in the market rules section.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Monitor timeline states

    Follow the status panel and countdown on the market page.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Keep evidence ready

    If a dispute is needed, gather clear, verifiable source material tied to market rules.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Edge-case reminders

- Some markets resolve quickly; others require extended review.
- Claiming is only available after final resolution state.
- If status seems inconsistent, contact support with market link and transaction hash.
