## What resolution means

Resolution is the final outcome decision for a market.
After resolution is finalized, winning positions become redeemable.

## Resolution timeline on market pages

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Outcome proposed

    The oracle workflow receives a proposed result.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Dispute window / final review

    A challenge window may apply before the result is finalized.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Final outcome

    The market is finalized and claims are enabled for winning positions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  On <SiteName />, this status appears directly in the market timeline panel
  (including dispute/final review states when available).
</Callout>
