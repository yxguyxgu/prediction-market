## Event vs market

- An <strong>event</strong> groups related questions.
- A <strong>market</strong> is the tradable question you buy/sell.
- Each market has outcomes (usually <strong>Yes</strong>/<strong>No</strong>) with live prices.

## What you see on a market page

- Current chance/probability for each outcome
- Price chart and recent trades
- Order book (buyers and sellers waiting at each price)
- Your open orders and position context

## Typical workflow

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Find a market

    Browse the home feed or search by topic.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Open details

    Enter the market page to inspect prices, chart, and liquidity.
  </Step>
  <Step>

    Select an outcome, choose order type, review cost/fees, and submit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
