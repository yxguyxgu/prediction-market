## Available order types

<Accordions type="single" collapsible>
  <Accordion title="Market order" value="market-order">
    Executes against available liquidity immediately. Best when speed matters more than exact price.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Limit order" value="limit-order">
    Lets you define the exact price. The order stays open until matched, canceled, or expired.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Execution preference (market orders)

In <strong>Settings → Trading</strong>, <SiteName /> supports:

- <strong>FAK (Fill and Kill)</strong>: fill what is available now, cancel the rest.
- <strong>FOK (Fill or Kill)</strong>: fill entire order immediately or cancel fully.

## Quick guidance

- Use <strong>market</strong> in fast-moving markets when immediate execution matters.
- Use <strong>limit</strong> when you want strict price control.
