## What is <SiteName />?

<SiteName /> is a peer-to-peer prediction market where users trade on the outcome of real-world events.
Prices move in real time and represent crowd-implied probabilities.

## How markets are structured

- An **event** is a topic (for example, an election or earnings release).
- A **market** is a tradable outcome question inside that event.
- Most markets expose **Yes/No** outcomes priced between **$0.00 and $1.00**.

## Price = probability

If a "Yes" share is trading at **$0.37**, the market is pricing that outcome at roughly **37% probability**.
As traders buy and sell, the probability updates.

## Payout basics

- Winning shares settle to **$1.00** per share at resolution.
- Losing shares settle to **$0.00**.
- You can usually exit before resolution by selling your position.

## Start in 3 steps

1. Create your account and connect your wallet.
2. Fund your account (wallet transfer, on-ramp, or direct USDC transfer).
3. Open a market, choose your order type, and submit your trade.
